Shen Chen (陈燊)

I am a Researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab, where I work on computer vision and machine learning. And my research interests are deepfake detection, face anti-spoofing and image retrieval.

I finished the master's study and obtained my M.S. degree from Xiamen University in 2022, advised by Prof. Rongrong Ji. Earlier, I received the B.S.degree from Fuzhou University in 2018.
